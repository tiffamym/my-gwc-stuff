Our Mission

Our mission is to create a platform in which users can easily find a location in the Bay Area to eat. Whether it’s a full-course meal, dessert, drink, or even a quick snack, you can find it all here. Users can filter the type of cuisine they want, the specific city, and dietary restrictions in order to get personalized results.
